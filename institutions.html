Empowering Our Farmers

Major Crops: Paddy, Wheat, Mustard, and Pulses are grown across Murubanda's fertile lands.

Irrigation Sources: The village is supported by wells, tube wells, and seasonal rainfall.

Farming Support: Subsidized seeds, fertilizer distribution, and training sessions are offered by the block agriculture office.

Market Access: Farmers sell their produce in local haats and Ramgarh mandi, ensuring fair pricing and quick turnover.

Government Schemes: PM-KISAN, Soil Health Card Scheme, and Crop Insurance schemes are actively promoted.
